Fraser's Hill, Pahang

Fraser’s Hill is a mountain village in Pahang, Peninsular Malaysia, known for its highland rainforest and extensive birdlife. Several nature paths, including Pine Tree Trail and Bishop Trail, cross jungle terrain sheltering migratory birds like the Siberian thrush. The area also harbors Malayan tigers and rare trig oak trees. Near the village is the Jeriau Waterfall. Paddle boats traverse calm Allan’s Water Lake.

Things to do here!

Jeriau Waterfall

A lot of place to play with water. Beautiful waterfall.

Pine Tree Trail

This trail is about 5 km long with difficult steeps and undulations. Hikers can see beautiful sceneries from the mountain peak.

Taman Awam Bukit Fraser

Hilltop forest park with a playground, tarmac walking track, mini-golf course & food court.

Bird Interpretive Center

Bird Watching is one of the most popular activities at Frasher's Hill. You can find information on trails, the history of Frashers Hill and information about birds
