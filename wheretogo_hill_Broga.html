Bukit Tabur, Kuala Lumpur

Broga Hill is one of the most popular hiking landmarks on the outskirts of KL, being just about 40 minutes outside of town. T he hike - if you can really call it that - is pretty easy-going whether you take the "premium" trail, or the free trail (which should continue straight directly into the forest after the old woman selling drinks at a corrugated metal hut).
